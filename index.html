Biosensor Analytics

1. Daily Activity & Physiological Overview

This dashboard provides a Small Multiples overview of aggregated daily metrics. It visualizes the interplay between movement (Steps), metabolic output (Calories), and physiological response (Heart Rate, Temperature).

2. Fitness Assessment: Heart Rate vs. Step Correlation

This visualization analyzes cardiovascular efficiency by correlating physical exertion (Steps) with Heart Rate (HR). By comparing the user's data against established physiological benchmarks, we can assess whether the heart rate remains within an athletic or average range during high-intensity intervals.

Tools: Use the Heatmap to see density and calorie intensity, or activate Benchmarks to evaluate fitness levels (Athletic vs. Unsporty).
